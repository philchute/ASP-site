## Understanding Gunpla Timelines

The Mobile Suit Gundam franchise spans many different universes and timelines. This is a summary of the timelines and the series and movies that are part of each timeline. There are also a lot of manga comics and various video games set in these universes.

### Future Century (FC)
The setting of Gundam G Gundam. It is set further in the future, where humanity has colonized the galaxy and is fighting for control of resources.
*   **Series:** 
* FC060: Mobile Fighter G Gundam (49 episodes, 1994-1995)
*   **Key Mobile Suits:** G Gundam, Gundam Exia.

### After Colony (AC)
The timeline of Gundam Wing, popular in the West for introducing many fans to the franchise.
*   **Series:** 
* AC195: Gundam Wing (49 episodes, 1995-1996)
* AC196: Endless Waltz (3 episodes/1 movie, 1997)
*   **Key Mobile Suits:** Wing Gundam, Deathscythe, Tallgeese.

### After War (AW)
The timeline of Gundam X.
*   **Series:** 
* AW15: Gundam X (39 episodes, 1996)
*   **Key Mobile Suits:** Gundam X, Gundam Dynames.

### Correct Century (CC)
The timeline of Turn A Gundam.
*   **Series:** 
* 2343-2345: Turn A Gundam (50 episodes/2 movies, 1999-2000)

### Cosmic Era (CE)
The setting of Gundam SEED. It reimagines many UC concepts for a new generation, focusing on the conflict between Naturals and Coordinators.
*   **Series:** 
* CE71-72: Mobile Suit Gundam SEED (50 episodes, 2002-2003)
* CE73-74: Mobile Suit Gundam SEED Destiny (50 episodes, 2004-2005)
* CE73: Mobile Suit Gundam SEED C.E. 73: Stargazer (3 episodes, 2006)
* CE75: Mobile Suit Gundam SEED Freedom (1 movie, 2024)
* CE74: Mobile Suit Gundam SEED Freedom Zero (TBA, TBA)
*   **Key Mobile Suits:** Strike Gundam, Freedom, Justice.

### Anno Domini (AD)
The timeline of Gundam 00. It is set in our future where three power blocs fight for control of solar energy.
*   **Series:** 
*  AD2307-2312: Mobile Suit Gundam 00 (50 episodes, 2007-2009)
*  AD2314: Mobile Suit Gundam 00: A Wakening of the Trailblazer (1 movie, 2010)
*   **Key Mobile Suits:** Exia, Dynames, 00 Raiser.

### Advanced Generation (AG)
The timeline of Gundam AGE.
*   **Series:** 
*  AG115-164: Mobile Suit Gundam AGE (49 episodes, 2011-2012)

### Regild Century (RG)
The timeline of Gundam Reconguista in G.
*   **Series:** 
*  RG1014: Mobile Suit Gundam Reconguista in G (26 episodes, 2014-2015)

### Post Disaster (PD)
The setting of Iron-Blooded Orphans. It takes place 300 years after a great war known as the "Calamity War."

### Ad Stella (AS)
The setting of Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch from Mercury.
*   **Series:** 
*  AS101: Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch from Mercury (1 episode, 2022)
*  AS122: Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch from Mercury (24 episodes, 2022-2023)

### Build Fighters (BF)
These series are set in the present day and are about kids building gunpla kits and competing in tournaments.
*   **Series:** 
*  Gundam Build Fighters (25 episodes, 2013-2014)
*  Gundam Build Fighters Try (25 episodes, 2014-2015)
*  Gundam Build Divers (25 episodes, 2018-2019)
*  Gundam Build Divers Re:Rise (26 episodes, 2019-2020)
*  Gundam Build Metaverse (3 episodes, 2023)
