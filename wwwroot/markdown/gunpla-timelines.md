## Guide to Gunpla Timelines

The first Gundam series, Mobile Suit Gundam, was released in 1979. Many subsequent series, movies, video games, novels, and manga comics have been released as part of the Gundam franchise. Many of these are sequels, prequels, side stories, or spin-offs of the original series. These are set in what's called the "Universal Century" timeline. Other Gundam series have been set in different timelines, sometimes farther in the future or otherwise different in technology or political pressures driving the plot. This is a summary of the timelines in the Gundam franchise:

### Universal Century (UC)
The original and most expansive timeline, starting with the original 1979 Mobile Suit Gundam, set during the 'One Year War' in the year UC0079. It features a "hard sci-fi" approach to war and politics.
**Key Mobile Suits:** RX-78-2, Zaku II, Nu Gundam, Unicorn.

### Future Century (FC)
The setting of Mobile Fighter G Gundam. It is set much further in the future, with more technologically advanced Mobile Suits. Humanity has colonized the galaxy and is fighting for control of resources.
**Key Mobile Suits:** Shining Gundam, G Gundam, Rising Gundam.
